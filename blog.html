My Post Title About Cryptology

Jennifer Petit / July 19th, 2022

Elizabeth S Friedman worked as a cryptanalyst for the U.S Navy from 1916, during the world war, till the day she passed away on October 31st 1980. She was the first female code breaker and one of her significant accomplishments was deciphering the enemies (the nazi) code in both World Wars,

and she also helped solve international smuggling cases during Prohibition.
