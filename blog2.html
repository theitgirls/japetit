What I did today...

Today me and my group made our own citizen science presentation. The goal of our project is to see if the weather affects the population of cardinals in the United States. The way we use technology is; the participants from all states to take photos of cardinals and send it to us along with the specific weather that day (sunny, shady, rain, or snow & temperature). We will compare the amount of cardinals in warmer weather in states (Florida, Arizona, and California) to the amount of cardinals in colder states (Alaska, Minnesota, and Montana)
